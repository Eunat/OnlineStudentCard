ABOUT ME

Why do you do Ironhack?

I’m a former researcher, passionate about behavior, digital disruption and innovative approaches. Thus, UX/UI design stroke me as the natural playfield to develop all my previous skills. Furthermore, as somebody always eager to learn, with an interdisciplinary, international profile (6 countries, 5 languages), Ironhack looked like the perfect fit. Vinted scholarship did the rest.

What do you look for in a company culture?

I’m looking for a company that fosters creativity, skills development and “out-of-the-box” thinking, with multidisciplinary teams and human, ethical and passionate management.

Superpower & why

First, the why: I’m utterly curious and willing to go to great lengths to learn anything worth the while. This has forged my superpower: I’m a very fast, analytical, adaptable learner.
